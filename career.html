Why Join Us?

If your desire is to work in an organization where learning is encouraged, skills are developed, and passion is rewarded, Diplomats Real Estate is exactly what you are looking for.

We grow talents and offer internship opportunities for students and graduates to learn from a world-class organization. Our internship programmes allow you to build technical expertise and personal skills to make an impact in your career through vital on-the-job experience.
